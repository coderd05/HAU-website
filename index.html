About BlueHope Foundation

\

\ BlueHope Foundation is a volunteer-run nonprofit focused on neighborhood education, family support services, and local small-grant programs. We run after-school tutoring, skills workshops, and partner with local organizations to fund community micro-projects.\

\
\
\
\

After-school tutoring

\

Free tutoring sessions, materials, and mentorship for students K-12.

\
\
\

Skills workshops

\

Career-readiness, digital skills, and financial literacy workshops for teens & adults.

\
\
\

Community micro-grants

\

Small grants to support neighborhood-led projects \'97 gardens, libraries, and events.
